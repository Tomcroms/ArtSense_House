ArtSense is a new way to appreciate and engage with art

The way we create artwork is changing. Were're no longer limited to traditionnal mediums. Instead, we now have the ability to create digital art using a variety of tools and technologies.

At ArtSense, we combine the talent of our IRL partner artists with digital technologies such as creative code, artificial intelligence and editing software. This allow us to create unique works of all kinds, including static images, 3D renderings, ineractive experiences and much more. We believe this new way of creating art is more than just a trend - it's the future. And we're excited to be at the forefront of this new frontier.

Speedy Graphito

The first artist to integrate the ArtSense incubator

Olivier Rizzo alias Speedy Graphito, one of the pioneers of the French "Street art" movement, marked an entire generation by imposing an innovative and striking style in the early 80s.

Speedy Graphito wants to experience art as a life experience. He explores its different facets, not hesitating to question his own artistic identity. His styles are numerous and reveal universes where the artist, guided by his instinct, likes to get lost in search of a possible evolution. As comfortable in public pictorial performances as in the intimacy of his studio, SG devotes himself body and soul to passion in the urgency of passing time.
